About Me

Innovative restauranteur and mixologist with over a decade of experience in the food and beverage industry, specializing in crafting innovative cocktails and providing comprehensive consultancy services to restaurants and bars.

Renowned for a minimalistic yet elegant approach to drink design and a deep understanding of flavor profiles, which has significantly enhanced customer satisfaction and business growth. My proven track record spans menu development, staff training, and operational improvements, coupled with a strategic mindset aimed at elevating the dining experience.

Currently serving as Food & Beverage Specialist and Restaurant Manager at Sunborn Saga Oy in Finland, I continue to push boundaries in hospitality excellence.
