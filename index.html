About

With over a decade of experience in the food and beverage industry, I am an innovative restaurateur and mixologist specializing in crafting exceptional cocktails and providing comprehensive consultancy services. My minimalistic yet elegant approach to drink design, combined with a deep understanding of flavor profiles, has significantly enhanced customer satisfaction and business growth across multiple establishments.

Currently based in Naantali, Finland, I bring a unique blend of Asian heritage and Nordic sensibilities to my work. My journey has taken me from Malaysia to Finland, allowing me to develop a truly international perspective on hospitality and mixology.
